Local Lead Integrity

One in five October leads originated outside Oklahoma. Maintaining a strong local signal keeps intake focused on the clients we can actively serve.

September Benchmark

Local Capture
80% In-state conversions
Refining geo cues can reclaim the remaining 1 in 5 inquiries.
Immediate refinements:
  • Elevate Oklahoma-first copy across LSAs, GBP, and hero sections.
  • Route non-local calls to referral partners or nurture sequences early.
  • Expand negative geo keywords and tighten service-area messaging.
