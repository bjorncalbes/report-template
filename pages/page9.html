Why Review Consistency Matters

Critical ranking factor for maps visibility and local prominence

Why this matters

Consistent review velocity signals to Google that your business is actively serving clients and maintaining high satisfaction levels. Locations with steady review growth rank higher in map pack results, appear more credible to potential clients, and maintain competitive advantage over firms with stagnant or declining review activity. Fresh reviews also provide social proof at the exact moment prospects are deciding which attorney to contact.

CLIENT ACTION ITEM

Create GBP for Another Key Target Location

Expand your local footprint by establishing a Google Business Profile in a strategic secondary market

Recommended Locations:

  • Santa Monica - High-value market with strong search volume for criminal defense attorneys
  • Beverly Hills - Premium market with affluent clientele seeking legal representation
  • Pasadena - Growing market with less competition than downtown LA

Setup Requirements:

  • Physical office location or service area business designation
  • Dedicated phone number for the location (can forward to main office)
  • Unique business hours if different from main location
  • Verification via postcard, phone, or email (method depends on Google's requirements)

Why Creating GBP for a Key Target Location Matters

Strategic expansion into high-value markets increases visibility and lead generation

Why this matters

Each additional Google Business Profile listing gives you another opportunity to dominate local map pack results in high-value markets. Google's local algorithm favors businesses with verified locations in the searcher's area, meaning a Santa Monica office listing will outrank distant competitors for "criminal defense attorney near me" searches from that neighborhood. Multiple strategic locations multiply your visibility, capture different geographic search volumes, and position your firm as an established regional authority rather than a single-location practice.
